Bitcoin DCA Cycle Simulator

Pick a start date and savings plan, then compare daily DCA with cycle-aware buying and trimming.

Cycle strategies wait with cash, then buy when BTC falls to or lower. They stop new buys when BTC reaches a fresh all-time high (ATH).

How it works

p-levels are supply cost-basis percentiles: the buy price of the BTC supply at that percentile. p50 means BTC is near the median coin cost basis; lower p-levels usually mean deeper market pain, higher p-levels usually mean heat.

All cycle strategies use the same buy rule: once the buy phase is active, buy the larger of the p-level grid amount or 0.5% of portfolio value per day. Cycle Buy + Trim sells BTC worth 0.5% of portfolio value per day near p95-p100.

Daily DCA uses one fixed daily order size based on a 31-day month. It only switches to 2x when enough cash remains to keep buying every day for about a month.
